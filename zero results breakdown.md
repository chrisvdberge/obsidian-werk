## Finding
**88.6%** of strict zero result searches are item searches, **11.4%** are term searches. Including spell suggestions this is 65.6% and 34.4%

**29.4%** of item strict zero result searches are unique. **45.2%** of term zero result searches are unique. Including spell suggestions this is 31.1% and 54.2%
